Bionicle Heritage

Introduction


Harken to my tale, young friends. Aeons ago, in the times of turmoil, wars and suffering The Great Spirits sent us, matoran, to the blackness of infinity. They couldn't bear the strife and deaths anymore, so they longed us to help the other heathen. And our purpose was noble and clear, and our wish was like a never fading flame. So we built this vessel - this magnificent arc to bring the heathen everything they needed. And generations passed, and our path didn't ended. We were ignited with faith of embracement a new home after arrival, and this made us delighted and filled with happiness. But nothings lasts forever. The Crash was foretold in tales and legends of the says so old, and our hope was shattering into the pieces like this arc. The tomb of fate and beliefs is what became our home we had dreamt of. And that we call Heritage. So for the sake of survival we will fight.
