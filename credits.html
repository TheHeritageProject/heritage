Island Guardians

First and second era


Ren · El-Motu Guardian

Ren lives on EL-Motu Island. Like other defenders, she does not have elemental abilities, but she has advanced skills in technology and mechanics. She wears one of the strongest armors among the defenders. Her mask allows her to survive outside the island's energy field and stay in the vacuum of space for approximately 30 minutes.


Island: El-Motu

Weapon: Space surf

Mask: Taurum

Paora · En-Motu Guardian

Paora lives on En-Motu Island. Like other defenders, he does not have elemental abilities, but he is skilled in weapons. His body structure is unique among the defenders, with insectoid legs. He wears a jetpack that enables him to fly and a mask that amplifies the voltage of his weapons and increases the flow of plasma from his blaster.


Island: En-Motu

Weapon: Plasma Blaster

Mask: Gaomi
